DeltaWerks Design System (Web Spec)
Aesthetic: "Spy-Fi / Industrial Future"
Core Visuals: Deep blacks, dark slate blues, semi-transparent glass panels, and high-contrast neon accents.
1. Color Palette
Core Backgrounds
Main Background: #000000 (Deep Void)
Card/Modal Background: #0F172A (Panel Dark) - Often used with opacity
Input/Secondary Background: #1E293B (Panel Mid)
Overlay/Dimming: rgba(0,0,0,0.5)
Typography Colors
Primary Text: #E5E7EB (Off-white, high readability)
Muted/Subtitle Text: #64748B (Slate Grey)
Accent: Cyan (Energy & Active States)
Glow/Hover: #67E8F9
Core Icons/Buttons: #22D3EE
Gradients: #06B6D4
Accent: Blue (Structure & Borders)
Secondary Text: #60A5FA
Focus Rings: #3B82F6
Borders/Dividers (CRITICAL): #2563EB (Often used at 60% opacity)
Button Hover Bg: #1D4ED8
Status
Warning: #FDE047 (Yellow)
Error: #FCA5A5 (Red)

2. Typography
Headings: Orbitron, sans-serif
Style: Futuristic, technical, rigid. Used for titles and data displays.
Body: Roboto, sans-serif
Style: Clean, readable. Used for paragraphs and nav links.
UI/Labels: Roboto Condensed, sans-serif
Style: Compact. Used for badges and dense UI.
Data: monospace
Style: Used for clocks, coordinates, and raw data.

3. Web Component Specs
Glass Panel (Cards & Containers)
Background: #0F172A at 70% opacity.
Border: 1px solid #2563EB at 60% opacity.
Blur Effect: backdrop-filter: blur(4px).
Border Radius: 12px.
Shadow: Large, blue-tinted shadow (0 25px 50px -12px rgba(37, 99, 235, 0.1)).
Primary Action Button ("Hollow Tech" Style)
Background: Transparent (default).
Border: 1px solid #1D4ED8 (Blue 700).
Text Color: #60A5FA (Blue 400).
Font Weight: Semi-bold (600).
Border Radius: 6px.
Hover State:
Background becomes rgba(29, 78, 216, 0.2).
Text becomes #93C5FD (lighter blue).
Navigation Bar
Position: Sticky at top (z-index: 50).
Background: Black at 50% opacity.
Blur: backdrop-filter: blur(4px).
Bottom Border: 1px solid #2563EB at 60% opacity.
Height: 64px.
Form Inputs
Background: #1E293B.
Border: 1px solid #475569.
Text Color: #E5E7EB.
Placeholder Color: #64748B.
Focus State: Blue border (#3B82F6) with matching ring.
